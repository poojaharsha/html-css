"Coming home" movie script

Act 1 Act 2 Act 3 Act 4 Studios

Description of my script: Its about relaization during a journey

Sagar, an Indian born Software Engineer has gone to visit India for a while and he tells his mom how much he misses India and his home. Mom and him have a conversation.

Act 1

Kiran Kapoor
He was born in India and has moved abroad after finishing his bachelors in engineering in India. He has done his masters in Texas and he is working in Software company there. Now he has gone to visit India for his cousin's marriage. He misses his home while he is in the US.
Aarthi
A typical Indian mother who loves her son and she misses her son too.

Act 2

The location is at character's home in Bombay, India. The characters are at his home kitchen.

Act 3

The plot is Sagar has met his relatives and his family at his cousin's marriage and has had a good time which he craved for in the US.

Now he has to go back to India in a few days and he is having a casual discussion with his mom about how he feels about going back.
Mom also expresses to him how much she misses him and asks him to make a decision soon.

Act 4

Conversation between mother and son

Kiran: Mom, I love working in the US but I miss you all a lot.
Aarthi: Son, we miss you too, I miss seeing you so much everyday, I still feel you are my child though you are now 25.
Kiran: Mom, this trip has made me realize life is all about relations and people around you and not the status or money you get while you work alone in another country.
Aarthi: Son, you are always welcome to come home, this is your home!
